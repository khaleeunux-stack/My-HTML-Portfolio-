The Best Online Courses According to Khalil

My top 3 Courses of all-time.

Web Development – Angela Yu (Udemy)

Angela Yu’s Complete Web Development Bootcamp is one of the most beginner-friendly and practical courses out there. From HTML, CSS, JavaScript to backend with Node.js and MongoDB, it gives you everything you need to become a full-stack developer.

Cybersecurity – Long Liang

Long Liang’s cybersecurity courses are focused, hands-on, and industry-aligned. He teaches ethical hacking, penetration testing, and practical network security—ideal for anyone serious about becoming a cybersecurity expert.

SEO Academy – Srini Raghava (Udemy)

Srini Raghava’s SEO Academy is a go-to resource for mastering technical SEO, WordPress optimization, and Amazon affiliate marketing. His clear teaching style and practical strategies make it easy to apply what you learn immediately.
